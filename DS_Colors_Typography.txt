COLORS & TYPOGRAPHY
The Colors and Typography elements of my Design System are driven by CSUF’s web style standards. There are three primary CSUF colors - Blue, White, and Orange. The text follows typographic principles to establish a visual hierarchy and maximize the legibility of content.
